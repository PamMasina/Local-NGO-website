Initiative for Community Gardens

Description: The purpose of establishing community gardens is to encourage wholesome eating, sustainable gardening, and community building.

Objectives:

  • Supply local households with fresh produce.
  • Inform the public about environmentally friendly gardening techniques.
  • Encourage community involvement by offering gardening classes.

Program for Youth Mentoring

Description: Assisting young people who are at-risk in their academic and personal growth by matching them with mentors.

Objectives:

  • Boost young people's academic achievement and self-esteem.
  • Provide direction and career counsel.
  • Plan social events for the group once a month to foster community.

Campaign for Environmental Cleanup

Description: Organizing neighborhood clean-up days to improve parks, rivers, and residential areas.

Objectives:

  • Encourage environmental care and enhance communal areas.
  • Mobilize volunteers among the community's residents of all ages.
  • Increase public awareness of trash management and littering.

Fair for Health and Wellbeing

Description: Organizing a health fair with free health screenings, wellness seminars, and guidance on leading a healthy lifestyle.

Objectives:

  • Raise community awareness of the health options available.
  • Make preventive healthcare services accessible.
  • Encourage people to adopt healthier lifestyles by holding interactive workshops.

Program for Senior Outreach

Description: Providing elderly persons with company and support, especially those who are isolated.

Objectives:

  • Alleviate loneliness by scheduling frequent interactions with seniors.
  • Provide support for routine activities and resource access.
  • Plan social gatherings to promote older citizen contact.

Program for Food Distribution and Drive

Description: Gathering and giving non-perishable food supplies to low-income families.

Objectives:

  • Reduce food insecurity in the neighborhood.
  • Collaborate with nearby businesses and educational institutions to gather food.
  • Plan frequent distribution activities to assist low-income families.
